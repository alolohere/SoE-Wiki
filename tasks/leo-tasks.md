# Leo Tasks

### Leo on Souls of Elysium <a href="#leo_on_souls_of_elysium" id="leo_on_souls_of_elysium"></a>

**Souls of Elysium** offers a storyline quest starting in Leo's Enclave!

* Leo's Enclave has a teleport to it from every mainland city Temple.
* Leo represents the forces of good in the battle for Souls Of Elysium.
* Leo assigns tasks, growing in danger and pushing you to fight stronger Demonic/Diabolic monsters!
* The book near Leo can be read to understand what tier of monsters you're needing to fight for your task.
