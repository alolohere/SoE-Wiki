# Premium Account

### Premium Account <a href="#premium_account" id="premium_account"></a>

**Souls of Elysium** offers some unique benefits for supporting the server with a Premium Account from the [Game Store](../cosmetics/store.md)!

* Premium accounts gain 1 Hourly token every hour they are logged in (on a level 20+ character) that can be redeemed for special prizes like experience boosts and other items, located in Darashia!
* Premium Account Characters gain a Skull Coin every Diabolic Monster they kill!
* By having a Premium account you can buy one of the available homes around the cities of Souls of Elysium.
* Premium accounts gain 10% more experience than free account players.
* Premium accounts can also found a Guild!
