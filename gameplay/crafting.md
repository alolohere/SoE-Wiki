# Crafting

In **Souls of Elysium** you can craft a variety of items to improve your character.

* Resources gained from [Mining](mining/) can be used to craft powerful equipment and upgrade items.
* Some rare items can be combined to more powerful variants.



### Crafting Stations <a href="#crafting_stations" id="crafting_stations"></a>

* Kazordoon, next to the DP.
