# Item Box

Item Box

**Item Box** can be obtained when you kill a [Diabolic Creature](../diabolic-demonic-monsters.md), or bought for 10 Skull Coins from the [Skull Coin](./) shop in Darashia.

When opened, each item box will give one out of 250 different items, **some** of which are listed below. Items in **bold** are rare.
