# Blue Crystal (Lava Dragon Access)

## The Blue Crystal (Demona) <a href="#the_blue_crystal_demona" id="the_blue_crystal_demona"></a>

**Level Required:** 110, Recommended 130+ with team, or 200+ with 1 shooter and 1 tank.\
**Prerequisites:** None\
**Rewards:** 1 Crystal Coin, 100,000 experience



* This quest is started by speaking with An Old Dragonlord in Draconia.
* It is possible to get the Blue Crystal **before** talking with An Old Dragonlord.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (633).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: hi

  TO MAINTAIN PEACE AND BALANCE. DUE TO OUR PASSIVE NATURE, MANY OF OUR KIND HAVE NOW PERISHED OR BEEN

  ZATHROTH BEFORE THE DARKNESS CONSUMES US ALL?

#### Directions <a href="#directions" id="directions"></a>

* Be prepared to meet Infernalists, Warlocks, Hellhounds, Hellfire Fighters, Demons and a Hellfire Mage at the end.
* Start by heading north east of Carlin to get to the Maze of Lost Souls.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (634).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Behind a tree two levels down is a portal to Demona.
* Once in Demona, follow this path.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (635).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (636).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Pull the lever and head up the tower.
* After defeating the Hellfire Mage on the top floor a teleport will appear.
* This will bring you to the reward room. Click the chest to receive the blue crystal ![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (638).png>)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (639).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After entering the portal you will appear in Ab'dendriel.
* This tile is now a shortcut to Draconia. Bring the crystal to An Old Dragonlord
*

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (640).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Player: hi
Player: mission
An Old Dragonlord: Did you get the blue crystal?
Player: yes
An Old Dragonlord: EXCELLENT (crushes the crystal, consuming its energy) SO MUCH POWER! I FEEL LIKE
  A YOUNG DRAGON ONCE MORE!!! THANK YOU HUMAN! YOUR NEXT MISSION IS TO ELIMINATE 400 LAVA DRAGONS.
  
```

## Eliminate Lava Dragons <a href="#eliminate_lava_dragons" id="eliminate_lava_dragons"></a>

**Prerequisites:** The Blue Crystal (Demona)

There are two spawns with Lava Dragons. One below An Old Dragonlord in Draconia and another in Fibula. The one in Fibula requires a golden key to enter.

```
Player: hi
An Old Dragonlord: GEETINGS MORTAL! HAS OIN SENT YOU? OR HAVE YOU COME TO ASK FOR A MISSION?
Player: mission
An Old Dragonlord: Did you kill 400 LAVA DRAGONS?
Player: yes
An Old Dragonlord: EXCELLENT... YOUR BLOOD IS TRULY THAT OF A DRAGON... MAKE YOUR PREPARATIONS AND ASSEMBLE
  YOUR TEAM.. THIS WILL BE YOUR HARDEST MISSION YET! WE MUST MAKE OUR MOVE! CONSUME THIS CRYSTAL AND YOU WILL
  HAVE THE ENERGY TO PASS THROUGH THE BARRIER! GO INTO THE CAVE BELOW AND DESTROY OUR FORMER BROTHEREN THAT
  HAVE FALLEN TO THE DARKNESS a dragon blood.
```

Consume the crystal by using it.

```
You feel a radiant energy rush through your body.
```

This will give you access to [**Zathroth**](zathroth.md)**.**
