#### Part two: Demonic Xerkar <a href="#part_twodemonic_xerkar" id="part_twodemonic_xerkar"></a>

After defeating Xerkar, head into the portal.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (134).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (135).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This area is filled with Demons, Hellfire Demons, Undead Dragons, Hellhounds, Draken Sentinel and Betrayed Wraiths. At the end of the area is the portal to **Demonic Xerkar**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (136).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (137).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The boss will teleport around regularly and you'll need to run around to find him again. When you defeated the boss the teleport will appear.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (138).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Go inside and take your reward.
