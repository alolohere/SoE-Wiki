# Blue Legs Quest

### Blue Legs Quest <a href="#blue_legs_quest" id="blue_legs_quest"></a>

Also known as the Dwarf King Quest.

**Level Required: 40**

* Bring a team of at least 2 players if you are level 50ish.
* Easily solo'd by 90+.
* You will encounter several Dragons, Giant Spiders, Wyrms, Hellspawns, a room full of Demon Skeletons and the Dwarf King himself.
* There is an exit portal right before the Dwarf Tower.
* Continue heading down the floors, once you get beyond the Tower it's pretty much a 1 way path to the King.
