# Infernatil

**Level Required:** 200\
**Prerequisites:** [Xerkar](xerkar.md)

#### Entrance <a href="#entrance" id="entrance"></a>

Similar to Xerkar the entrance to Infernatil is in the Edron Hero cave. You will pass the Demon Helmet Quest.

#### Dungeon <a href="#dungeon" id="dungeon"></a>

The path to the boss is filled with Many Faces, Hellfire Demons, Undead Dragons, Demons, Juggernauts, Wight and Lost Souls.

It is fairly straightforward. Just follow the path one floor at a time.
