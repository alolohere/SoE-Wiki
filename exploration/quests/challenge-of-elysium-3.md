# Challenge of Elysium 3

**Level Required:** 80

**Level recommended**: 100+



Location:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (652).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



Opening the Entrance:

The entrance is blocked by magic walls. In order to remove them, the corpse of a diabolic snake must be sacrificed.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (653).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



The Dungeon:

When entering the dungeon you will start at the top of a set of stairs.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (654).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Down the stairs you will face two **Enraged Witch** and one **Enraged Banshee**. At the end of the room is another **Enraged Witch**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (655).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Up the stairs is a relatively open room with an **Enraged Ancient Scarab**, **Enraged Minotaur Mage** and a pillar shooting energy. At the end of the room is an **Enraged Bygone Beholder**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (656).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The portal will lead you to the room that you can see to the south. To proceed you must kill the **Enraged Quara Predator**. A new portal will appear once it is killed.

There is another portal in the room with a blackboard that says “Retreat”, but I don't know where it leads.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (657).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The portal will lead you to an empty corridor.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (658).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Up the stairs is a small corridor and then a bridge with more pillars shooting energy. On the way you will meet three **Enraged Banshee**, one **Enraged Beholder** and one **Enraged Wyrm**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (659).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Down the stairs you will meet the first boss, **Nephthys**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (660).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Prepare yourselves before entering the next room. You will be met by six **Enraged Lizard Sentinel**.

Along the way is two **Enraged Ancient Scarab** and two **Enraged Witch**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (661).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Up the stairs is the next boss, **Amentuankh**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (662).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When entering the portal you will face one **Enraged Ancient Scarab** and two **Enraged Bygone Beholder**. Further down the corridor is **Two Behemoths** and one **Enraged Behemoth**.\
At the end you will face the last boss, **Soris**, accompanied by two **Enraged Bygone Beholder**. There is a set of stairs further into the room that can provide protection but there are additional **Enraged Ancient Scarab**, **Enraged Banshee** and **Behemoth** on the way.\
After defeating the boss the teleport will lead you to the reward room.<br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (663).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



Rewards:

1\. A Royal Helmet and 150 000 experience.

2\. Blessing scroll

3\. A winged backpack (Contains one outfit doll, two gem boxes, one amulet of loss, 7 gold ingots)
