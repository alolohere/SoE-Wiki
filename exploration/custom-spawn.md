# Custom Spawn

### Custom Spawns Location Guide on Souls of Elysium <a href="#custom_spawns_location_guide_on_souls_of_elysium" id="custom_spawns_location_guide_on_souls_of_elysium"></a>

**Souls of Elysium** contains dozens of custom spawns, introducing custom creatures, and familiar ones in a new environment!

* Not all spawns are made equally, some are harder to get to or contain more variety.
* Some Custom Monsters can be quite dangerous, so be careful!
* _Note: Entrance will be marked by cross-hair._

West of Carlin in the Ghostlands is a spawn with Mutated Humans, Mutated Rats, Mutated Tigers, Crypt Shamblers and Grim Reapers. Starts on -4.<br>

### Goroma <a href="#goroma" id="goroma"></a>

To reach the custom spawns on Goroma, go west via the hidden passage. Then down the stairs to the north west.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (544).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

#### Bonebeasts <a href="#bonebeasts" id="bonebeasts"></a>

**Creatures:** Bonebeasts, Crypt Shamblers, Demon Skeletons and two Water Elementals.

### Draken Island <a href="#draken_island" id="draken_island"></a>

Accessible from Port Hope, South of Chor. Find Drox in the harbor and ask for passage.

***

#### Souleaters <a href="#souleaters" id="souleaters"></a>

The entire northern part of the island is filled with Souleaters. Both on the ground level and up the mountain.

**Creatures:** Souleaters, Mutated Rats, Death Blobs, Mutated Bats, Mutated Tigers.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (548).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

#### Draken Tower <a href="#draken_tower" id="draken_tower"></a>

**Creatures:** Drakens, Lizard Chosen and Corrupted Spiders.
