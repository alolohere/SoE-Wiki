# Dungeons

## Dungeons <a href="#dungeons" id="dungeons"></a>

**Souls of Elysium** features a unique Dungeon system allowing players to queue into an instanced dungeon, fight monsters and hunt down a boss for rewards! Be careful as it features strong monsters and dangerous areas!

Dungeons can be done solo, or in a party and offer a variety of rewards!

* The Dungeon window is opened in game by clicking on the Dungeon icon in your window hotbar.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (82).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* The entrance fee for Dungeons are Dungeon Tokens, obtained by completing the second tier of Tasks or by trading with other players.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (83).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After completing a Dungeon, there is a difficulty selection, allowing you to increase the difficulty of the entire dungeon, but increasing the rewards you obtain as well! Each difficulty tier you complete unlocks the next one, culminating in quite the challenge!

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
