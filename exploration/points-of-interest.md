# Points of Interest

Points of Interest

**Souls of Elysium** has a unique collection mechanic, allowing you to obtain experience for exploring!

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (736).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (735).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (737).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Recognizable by their shimmering light, _Points of Interest_ are hidden ALL over!
* Right click on the rock to gain some experience. They can come in different shapes and colors, so keep an eye out!
* Holy Spawn in the FH Expansion Zone requires 55 Points of Interest to access.

High Scores on each server:



* [Sanctum](https://us.soerpg.com/sub.php?page=highscores\&type=11\&vocation=-1)<br>
* [Aedis](https://eu.soerpg.com/sub.php?page=highscores\&type=11\&vocation=-1)<br>
* [Mortalis](https://mortalis.soerpg.com/sub.php?page=highscores\&type=11\&vocation=-1)
