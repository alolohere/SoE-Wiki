# Falcon Monastery Island

* Level Required: 100
* Level recommended: 150+ and some team for last mission.
* Items Required: None

```
Completing this quest grant access to: Falcons Island and access to Oberon.
```

Go find **Gregor** located at the Knights Area in Thais at +1 floor. Ask him about mission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (685).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (686).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: Hi
Gregor: Greetings, Player.
Player: Mission
Gregor: Go talk to Avar Tar in Edron. He can help you furthur please go visit him.
```

Go find Avar Tar at the east gate of Edron. Ask him about a mission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (687).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (688).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: Hi
Avar Tar: Greetings, Player.
Player: Mission
Avar Tar: Falcons? I suggest you speak to Dalbrect in carlin he can sail you there.
```

Now it's time go to the **Port North** of Carlin and find Dalbrect. He's located at the west of Carlin on a small boat.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (689).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (690).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: Hi
Dalbrect: Be greeted, traveller Player. Welcome to my hut.
Player: Mission
Dalbrect: Before I can take you to the Falcon Isle I will need to give you a test to prove your strength.
          Retrieve a missing item deep under Edron and bring to back to me.
```

Go straight to Edron at the hero cave and get a Bag of oriental spices for Dalbrect. You will find it here:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (691).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Be prepared to face:

* Heros
* Princess
* Elder Beholder
* Falcon knight

Follow the next path:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (692).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (693).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (694).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Here you will face two Falcon Knights.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (695).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
20:08 You have found 1 bag of oriental spices, and gained 70000 experience.
```

Go back to **Dalbrect** at Carlin and report the mission.

```
Player: Hi
Dalbrect: Be greeted, traveller Player. Welcome to my hut.
Player: Mission
Dalbrect: Amazing! You have proven yourself worthy. Just say Falcon and I'll take you to the isle.
```

Now you can access **Falcons Island and it's bosses.**

&#x20;For the next mission you will need to get a tank and a good team. You will face a lot of **Falcon Knight and Falcon Paladin**.

For your last mission you will need to go back to the **Knights Area** in **Thais**. Speak with Gregor and ask him about a mission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (696).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: Hi
Gregor: Greetings, Player.
Player: Mission
Gregor: Your mission is to kill the 3 falcon commanders.
```

Now go to the **Falcon Island** and kill the 3 Falcon Commanders. They are located at the stars on the minimap.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (697).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The bosses are:

* Preceptor Lazare
* Grand Canon Dominus
* Grand Commander Soeren

While doing the boss to the south west, behind the building is a chest with a Forbidden Tome. Make sure to take it before leaving the island.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (698).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When you are done with the bosses and have the tome, go back to **Gregor** in Thais and report the mission.

```
Player: Hi
Gregor: Greetings, Player.
Player: Mission
Gregor: Excellent work citizen. Now listen closely. Return to Falcon Isle and retrive the sealed spellbook and return it to me!
```

Report again and hand in the Forbidden Tome.

```
Player: Hi
Gregor: Greetings, Player.
Player: Mission
Gregor: Excellent work citizen! I will grant you access to the lower floors on Falcon Isle!
```

This finished the quest.
