# Dark Thais

* Level Required: 100
* Level recommended: 200+ with team and tank.
* Items Required: None
* Completing this quest grant access to: Dark Thais, Demobats, Demodogs and ability to kill **Vecna**.



Find **Mike** located at the near the boat in west Thais. Ask him for a mission.

* He will ask you to find Patrick, near the Temple in Dark Thais.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (419).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Be prepared to face:

* Mutated Bats
* Souleaters
* Yielothax
* Many Face (on -1 floors and +1)



* Patrick's location its at the Dark Thais temple +1. You will find a death body that give you the next mission. There is one Many Face upstairs.

* Go back to **Mike** and report mission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (422).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Head back to Dark Thais and find Dustin.

* Asking Dustin for a mission, he asks you to slay 300 Many Faces.
* Its recommended to bring a high level team with good equipment and with a good tank. Many Faces are strong monsters.
* After slaying 300 Many faces, report back to Dustin.



* Find Eddie after Dustin's mission.

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (425).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (426).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Eddie will ask you to kill 400 Demobats. They are located near Eddie or in various places in Dark Thais.



* After completing Eddie's mission, seek out Hopper.

* Joyce asks you to kill Vecna, located to the North.
* To slay **Vecna** we recommend:
  * A team of 5 or more.
  * One or two Druids.
  * A Knight 250+ with solid gear.
