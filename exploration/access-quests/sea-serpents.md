# Sea Serpents

### Sea Serpent's Hunt <a href="#sea_serpent_s_hunt" id="sea_serpent_s_hunt"></a>

Level Required: 0

Items Required: 5 fish, 15 meat, 20 ham, 20 dragon ham

Rewards: Sea Serpent's access area

Quest starts on Svargrond.

Monsters: None

_Spoiler brought to you by Yureev._

Note: You can buy all items required at Jezzara in Ankrahmun Jezzara is located +1 from the green circle (beside the bank)

***

#### Solution <a href="#solution" id="solution"></a>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (680).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

First step is to travel to Svargrond and walk down from the boat, and then upstairs to the south boat.

Once you go up this ladder, you will start talking to Captain Haba.![](https://wiki.soerpg.com/lib/exe/fetch.php?tok=4a9c21\&media=https%3A%2F%2Fimgur.com%2FY2vNJTW.png)

***

#### Captain Haba <a href="#captain_haba" id="captain_haba"></a>

```
  Player: hi
  Captain Haba: Harrr, landlubber wha'd ya want?
  Player: mission
  Captain Haba: Ya wanna join the hunt fo' the sea serpent? Be warned ya may pay with ya life! Are ya in to it?
  Player: yes
  Captain Haba: A'right, we are here to resupply our stock of baits to catch the sea serpent. Your first task is to bring me 5 fish they are easy to catch. When you got them ask me for a mission again.
```

```
  Player: hi
  Captain Haba: Harrr, landlubber wha'd ya want?
  Player: mission
  Captain Haba: Excellent, now bring me 15 pieces of meat.
```

```
  Player: hi
  Captain Haba: Harrr, landlubber wha'd ya want?
  Player: mission
  Captain Haba: Excellent, now bring me 20 hams.
```

```
  Player: hi
  Captain Haba: Harrr, landlubber wha'd ya want?
  Player: mission
  Captain Haba: Excellent, now bring me 20 dragon hams.
```

```
  Player: hi
  Captain Haba: Harrr, landlubber wha'd ya want?
  Player: mission
  Captain Haba: Excellent, that should be enough fish to make the bait. Tell me when ya're ready fo' the hunt.
```

Now, we have access to ask him about 'hunt', which will be our way into Sea Serpents.
