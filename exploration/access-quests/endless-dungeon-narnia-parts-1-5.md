# Endless Dungeon (Narnia Parts 1-5)

### Endless Dungeon <a href="#endless_dungeon" id="endless_dungeon"></a>

* The Endless Dungeon is the first step towards accessing huge custom content.
* It is necessary to access the location _Narnia_ and the endgame equipment of the _Frosthaven location_.
* The location consists of multiple stages.
* It offers excellent spots for both solo farming and party play.

#### Access <a href="#access" id="access"></a>

* The Endless Dungeon is located to the west of Darashia

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (85).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* In the Archaeologists' Shelter, you will find the NPC Excavator

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (86).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* You must give 15 Perfect Behemoth Fangs to the Excavator to gain access to the Endless Dungeon.
* To obtain behemoth fangs, there are two methods - either _loot_ them from behemoths or _skin_ behemoths.
* To skin behemoths, you need an Obsidian Knife, available from Bo.
* _There is no need to take the quest. It grants access upon the first conversation if you have the required number of behemoth fangs._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (87).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (88).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Congratulations! You now have access to The Endless Dungeon**

#### Level 1. Sand <a href="#level_1_sand" id="level_1_sand"></a>

* The Endless Dungeon is divided into multiple floors.
* For quick access, there is the option to teleport via the elevator, but with one condition - _you must complete the path at least once_.
* At the end of each floor, you will encounter a boss to defeat to unlock elevator access.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (89).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Monsters: demon skeletons, ancient scarabs, liches, and bonebeasts.
* The level is not difficult, but for comfortable solo experience, recommend a minimum level of 80-100.
* Also, be cautious - the mobs are paralysis-heavy

#### Level 3. Toxic <a href="#level_3_toxic" id="level_3_toxic"></a>

From this point onward, it is highly recommended to have a full team with a blocker for further progression. This level is inhabited by creatures known as _Abominations_, and they are protected by _Worm Priestesses_. Recommended levels are 150-200, along with good equipment and team coordination. It is a popular spot for party farming due to the good experience and loot it offers.
