# Zao Lizards

### Zao City Access <a href="#zao_city_access" id="zao_city_access"></a>

* To begin, head to Chor, located south east of Port Hope.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (683).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Ask the sailor **Drox** for Passage.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (684).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Speak with the **Gate Guardian** outside the city located above.

```
Player: Hi
Gate Guardian: Chhhhzzz. Ugly humanzzz! You better have a good reazzzon to be here!
Player: Mission
Gate Guardian: You want entry into this city? I need you to do some community service first. 
               Kill 4000 of these monsters outside the city. Do you accept?
Player: Yes
Gate Guardian: Great, Go kill 4000 monsters in these desolate plains.
```

* The monsters do not need to be killed in this area, they can be killed anywhere.
* Monsters that count toward this quest are Ghastly Dragons, Drakens, Corrupted Spiders, Souleaters etc that you can see outside the city.

```
Player: Hi
Gate Guardian: Chhhhzzz. Ugly humanzzz! You better have a good reazzzon to be here!
Player: Mission
Gate Guardian: Have you finished your service?
Player: Yes
Gate Guardian: Good job, here is your reward. But, before you may enter, you will have to slay 
               the dragon to the north.                
               
```

\* Head to the North West Lizard Chosen spawn and enter the door to fight the boss.

Rewards:

* 1 Upgrade Crystal
* Access to the Draken City
* Ability to take a boat from Port Hope to Chor
