# Dream World

To receive access to quest, you need to complete [Steve Tasks.](steve.md)

As befits a dream, if you die in this place, you will wake up in Rookgaard temple without death panalty.

You may encounter along the way Enraged monsters as Orcs, Wasps, Poison Spiders and Witches at the end.
