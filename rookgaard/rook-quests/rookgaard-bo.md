# Rookgaard Bo

Requires access to the [Elf Tower](elf-tower.md) to start.



Speak to Oronvar for a mission.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (853).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

```
Player: hi
Oronvar: Welcome to my room.
Player: mission
Oronvar: Bo will only talk to humans he trusts. I can vouch for you but I will test your gathering skills first. Do we have a deal?
Player: yes
Oronvar: Okay! I'm trying to make a simple potion could you gather me 30 honeycombs and 20 bear paws?
Player: Yes
```

He will ask you to gather 30 honeycombs and 20 bear paws.

```
Player: Mission
Oronvar: Have you gathered the honeycombs and all the bear paws?
Player: yes
Oronvar: Very much appreciated.
"You gained 1500 experience points." 
Next step
Player: mission
Oronvar: Can you gather just a few more things for me?
Player: yes
Oronvar: Please bring me 50 minotaur leather. You will need a tool to skin them with so take this with you. (Given obsiidian knife)
When you return:
Player: mission
Oronvar: Did you bring me 50 minotaur leather?
Player: yes
Oronvar: Thank you. These will be of great use to me. I have one last thing I need. Talk to me when you are ready.
You gained 3200 experience points.
```

Ask for another mission.

```
Player: mission
Oronvar: I need some rare minerals, I will tell you where to find them but it may be dangerous. Are you interested?
Player: yes
Oronvar: Deep underground, somewhere beneath the island, lives a giant mole that hoards all of its precious gems...
Oronvar: Please go and bring back 30 topaz, 30 diamonds, and 30 amethyst from his lair.

```

He asks you to collect 30 topaz, 30 diamonds and 30 amethyst.

Diamonds and amethysts can also be obtained from the Caskets obtained from fishing, topaz can drop from demonic/diabolic rotworms.

```
Player: hi
Player: mission
Oronvar: Do you have the rare gems with you?
Player: yes
Oronvar: <pockets the gems> Well you have proven yourself more than worthy, I will introduce you to Bo.
```



After returning with the gems you will complete the quest.

Rewards: 12,500 experience

Access to Bo.
