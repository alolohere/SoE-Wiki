# Rook Shops

## Rookgaard Shops <a href="#rookgaard_shops" id="rookgaard_shops"></a>

On top of the regular NPCs shops located on the city and the surroundings there are also some “secret” NPCs that will sell or buy valuable items on the island.

### Bo <a href="#bo" id="bo"></a>

**Location:** Elf Area (top of the tower close to the Training Dummy)

**Requirements:** Buying Bo access on the Premium Shop (100 premium points)

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (32).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Trading list**

Bo, basically buys most of the creature products dropped from Rookgaard monsters (e.g: Minotaur leathers, minotaur horns…). Make sure to loot them while hunting since you will be able to sell them and make good profits.

### Cotton <a href="#cotton" id="cotton"></a>

**Location:** Door behind NPC Obi

**Requirements:** Access to the secret shop

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (41).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Gaining Access**

To access the secret shop, just ask NPC Obi about it

```
hi - secret shop
```

Obi will redirect to NPC Nevik (the fisherman), talk with Nevik about it, and after a 20k payment you will gain access to the secret shop.

**Trading list**

Cotton, basically buys valuable equipment dropped from monsters and bosses at really good prices, here is the list of items that this NPC buys. Most of the times you will get more profit by selling these items to other players.
