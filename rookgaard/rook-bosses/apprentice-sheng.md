# Apprentice Sheng

Apprentice Sheng is the boss of the minotaurs in Rookgaard. It spawns randomly (typically only 1 per day)

* Prerequisites: None

#### Strategies <a href="#strategies" id="strategies"></a>

Treat it like a a Demonic / Diabolic Minotaur Mage with more HP, at level 40+ you should be able to kill Apprentice Sheng by healing constantly.

Keep in mind that he will spawn with 2 Hyenas so they might break your shield.

#### Loot <a href="#loot" id="loot"></a>

Apprentice Sheng loot may vary from some platinum coins, gold ingot, etc.
