# Store

### Store Products on Souls of Elysium <a href="#store_products_on_souls_of_elysium" id="store_products_on_souls_of_elysium"></a>

* **Souls of Elysium** offers several features via the store functions.
* You'll be able to interact with the system via the store icon.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (9).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Scrolls <a href="#scrolls" id="scrolls"></a>

| Item Name          | Cost in Premium Currency | Notes                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 30 Day Prem Scroll | 50                       | 30 days of Premium                                                                                                                     |
| 60 Day Prem Scroll | 90                       | 60 days of Premium                                                                                                                     |
| 90 Day Prem Scroll | 120                      | 90 days of Premium                                                                                                                     |
| Mailbox Scroll     | 15                       | Creates a temporary mailbox for 30 seconds, includes a parcel and label.                                                               |
| Trainer Scroll     | 5                        | Lets you summon a trainer for 24 hours. Timer starts from the moment you use the scroll. “!train” and “!train check” are the commands. |
| Fishing License    | 100                      | Adds 8 repetitions to fishing. Usable once.                                                                                            |
